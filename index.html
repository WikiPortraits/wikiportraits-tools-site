About WikiPortraits

WikiPortraits is a project dedicated to improving Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects by capturing freely-licensed photos of people and cultural events. We help get Wikimedia photographers credentialed to events as press photographers, provide guidance on uploading, and support new contributors.

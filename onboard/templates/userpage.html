Create or Update Your User Page

Having a basic user page helps build trust in the Wikimedia community. It shows who you are and your connection to WikiPortraits.

Important: User pages should not be promotional. Keep it simple! We recommend writing in first person.

Open my user page Read user page guidelines
What to include:

A brief introduction about yourself written in first person. You can share your name, photography interests, that you joined through WikiPortraits, etc.

Remember: Keep it non-promotional.

Tip: When saving, you'll be asked for an edit summary where you describe your changes. Can be something like "Creating my user page".

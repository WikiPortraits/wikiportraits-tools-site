Set Your Attribution Name

By default, your photos will be attributed to your username. If you chose a pseudonym but want your uploads credited with your real name, you can change this in your preferences.

If your username is already how you want to be credited, skip this step and continue.

Open my preferences
How to change it:
  1. Click the link above to open your preferences
  2. Ensure you are on the "Upload Wizard" tab
  3. Find the "Author's name" field
  4. Enter your preferred name for attribution
  5. Save your changes
